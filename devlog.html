Devlog

Public development updates for Kelaris.

This page gives the project a professional update trail instead of a placeholder section. It works as a foundation for public milestones now and for fuller dev posts later.

Update 01
Project foundation

Kelaris moved from concept into active production with its visual direction, gameplay pillars, and long-term identity defined.

Update 02
Official website and community launch

The public site and Discord are now live, establishing a central hub for updates, community growth, and future launch visibility.

Update 03
Core systems development

Work continues on the foundations that power the colony, world simulation, progression flow, and the long-form survival loop.

Update 04
Interface and player experience

UI and front-end presentation are being shaped to support readability, clarity, and a strong first impression across both game and website.

Update 05
Public-facing structure is in place

The site is now organized into dedicated pages for the game, media, links, and devlog, creating a cleaner foundation for future content drops.

Current focus

What Kelaris is building toward

World and expansion flow

Developing a world that supports discovery, territory pressure, and strategic growth over time.

Colony and automation systems

Strengthening the systems that let the colony scale from fragile survival to reliable industrial momentum.

Defense, UI, and polish

Improving the player-facing experience so the game reads clearly and feels more professional as systems become visible.
