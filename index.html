The pitch

From emergency landing to industrial survival

Build a living colony

Lay out structures, manage priorities, and grow from a fragile outpost into a settlement that can support expansion without collapsing under its own complexity.

Scale through automation

Turn manual survival into momentum with layered production, logistics, and infrastructure that keep the colony moving even under pressure.

Defend what you create

Growth attracts danger. Expansion brings opportunity, but it also pushes your colony closer to the edge if your defenses and planning fall behind.
