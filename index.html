What is Kelaris?

Build a living colony

Lay out structures, create efficient production flow, assign priorities, and shape a settlement that grows from a fragile foothold into a self-sustaining base.

Survive a hostile world

Face environmental pressure, logistical strain, limited resources, and external threats that force adaptation instead of passive expansion.

Scale through systems

Kelaris is about layering mechanics together: exploration, automation, crafting, defense, and long-term colony planning all feed into one another.

Core pillars

Colony management Oversee workers, priorities, and expansion planning while balancing immediate survival needs with long-term growth.
Resource and production loops Gather raw materials, refine them, power your infrastructure, and build efficient chains that support a growing population.
Defense and pressure Prepare for threats, strengthen your perimeter, and respond to danger without collapsing your internal economy.
Exploration Push outward into the unknown, uncover terrain, discover opportunities, and adapt your strategy to the world around you.
Systems-driven gameplay Expect interacting mechanics instead of isolated features, letting your choices create the story of each playthrough.
Long-form progression Move from fragile beginnings to high-complexity colony operations with meaningful milestones along the way.

About This Game

Establish humanity’s foothold on a hostile alien world. Build a living colony, automate complex systems, and defend against escalating threats as every expansion pushes you deeper into the unknown.

Kelaris is a colony survival game about pressure, growth, and control.
Build a living colony Start with a fragile outpost and grow it into a self-sustaining stronghold. Plan efficient layouts, manage priorities, and expand without letting complexity break your colony.
Automate everything Turn manual survival into industrial momentum. Build layered production chains, process raw resources, and scale your infrastructure into a machine that can run under pressure.
Explore a dangerous world Push into unknown territory, secure new resources, and adapt to the terrain around you. Expansion creates opportunity — but it also creates risk.
Defend under pressure Growth attracts danger. Build defenses, respond to threats, and keep your colony functioning when the world starts pushing back.
Systems that interact Logistics, production, power, survival, and expansion all feed into one another. Small mistakes grow into major problems, and smart planning creates long-term stability.
From outpost to machine Begin with survival. End with mastery. Build a colony that can scale, adapt, and endure.

Survival is expected. Mastery is earned.
