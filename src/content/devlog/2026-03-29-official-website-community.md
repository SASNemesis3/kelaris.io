Kelaris now has a public home.

The website gives the project dedicated pages for the game overview, feature pillars, media, links, contact, account access, and development updates. The Discord link also gives the community a central place to gather while the game is still in development.

This stage is about public structure. Even before the site has trailers, press kits, or release pages, it needs a clean foundation that can support them later.

The site is intended to feel like Kelaris: dark, atmospheric, sci-fi, practical, and focused on survival systems instead of generic marketing polish.
