Kelaris is moving from idea into a more organized public project.

The first foundation work focuses on defining the tone of the game, the public-facing identity, and the systems-first survival fantasy behind the project.

The goal is not just to have a placeholder page. The goal is to build a central home for the game that can grow alongside development: devlogs, media, public roadmap updates, member access, and future launch pages.

Core identity remains simple:

- Build a living colony.
- Scale through automation.
- Defend what you create.
- Keep the machine running when pressure hits.
