Core features

Every major system feeds the same colony story.

Kelaris is built around connected gameplay pillars. Nothing is meant to feel isolated. Your layout affects logistics, logistics affect survival, survival affects expansion, and expansion changes the pressure on everything else.

Gameplay pillars Build. Automate. Expand. Defend. Kelaris is strongest when these loops work together instead of separately.
Colony management

Assign priorities, structure growth, and make sure the colony keeps functioning as new demands and bottlenecks appear.

Production and logistics

Gather raw materials, process them into useful outputs, and shape production flow that supports expansion instead of slowing it down.

Automation

Replace constant manual intervention with layered systems that keep the settlement productive under stress.

Exploration

Push beyond the safety of your first landing zone to secure resources, open routes, and uncover new strategic opportunities.

Defense and response

Protect the colony from escalating pressure without destroying the internal economy needed to survive long-term.

Replayable progression

Each playthrough should tell a different story through changing priorities, expansion paths, and the way systems collide.

What players will be managing

  • Space, layout, and colony flow
  • Resource intake and production stability
  • Risk versus reward when expanding outward
  • Defensive readiness without overcommitting
  • The long-term health of a growing colony

What makes the loop compelling

  • Small mistakes can grow into major colony problems
  • Good planning creates room for stronger scaling
  • Expansion creates both opportunity and vulnerability
  • Pressure turns success into a moving target
  • The best colony is not the biggest — it is the one that holds together
