# Roadmap and Press Kit Notes

This branch adds the first public-facing roadmap and press kit foundation for the Kelaris website.

## Roadmap

The roadmap is intentionally public-facing. It should communicate direction without promising exact dates or exposing internal production details.

## Press Kit

The press kit is a foundation for future factsheets, screenshots, key art, trailers, logos, and downloadable press assets.

## Identity Rule

Keep the site aligned with the Kelaris identity: dark sci-fi atmosphere, practical colony-survival language, glass-panel UI, and blue/teal accent colors.
