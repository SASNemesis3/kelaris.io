About This Game

Establish humanity’s foothold on a hostile alien world.

Build a living colony, automate layered systems, and defend your growing settlement as every expansion pushes you deeper into risk. Kelaris is a PC colony survival game about turning instability into control — and keeping that control when the world starts resisting you.

Start fragile

You begin with a vulnerable foothold and limited capacity. The early game is about making smart decisions fast and building enough stability to survive the next problem.

Scale with intent

Expansion is not just about building more. It is about building better — better flow, better logistics, better defensive coverage, and better long-term resilience.

Build a self-sustaining machine

As the colony grows, Kelaris shifts from direct management toward system design. Smart infrastructure is what allows late-stage survival to feel earned.

Hold under pressure

Danger, scarcity, and expansion stress are all part of the challenge. The colony must keep working when things go wrong, not just when everything is calm.

What defines the experience

  • Build and optimize a growing colony
  • Manage systems that become more demanding over time
  • Explore outward to secure territory and resources
  • Balance industrial growth with survival pressure
  • Create long-term stability through strong planning

The fantasy of Kelaris

You are not just surviving another day. You are engineering a colony that can outlast the environment, absorb setbacks, and keep expanding with confidence.

Survival is expected. Mastery is earned.
