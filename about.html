About Kelaris

A colony survival game built around pressure, growth, and control.

Kelaris is a top-down sci-fi strategy experience for PC where the player must build order out of chaos. The fantasy is not just survival — it is turning a dangerous foothold into a stable, expanding machine that can survive its own scale.

Vision Systemic survival Kelaris is designed around interacting systems that reward planning, adaptation, and strong colony management.

What Kelaris is

A colony survival sim with a heavy focus on logistics, expansion, automation, and defensive pressure. Every system is meant to connect back into the same larger colony story.

  • Build and organize a living settlement
  • Push outward to secure space and resources
  • Scale from manual survival to industrial stability
  • Hold the colony together when the world pushes back

What kind of experience it aims for

Kelaris is for players who enjoy depth, long-form decision-making, and systems that create stories through consequences rather than scripted moments.

  • Readable strategy with layered complexity
  • Meaningful escalation instead of flat progression
  • A colony that feels alive because its systems interact
  • Replay value driven by the way worlds and colonies evolve
Core identity

Three principles shaping the project

Readable at a glance

The colony should be understandable in motion. A player ought to recognize what is working, what is breaking, and what matters next.

Deep through interaction

Depth comes from systems feeding into one another — workers, resources, layout, expansion, and defense all influence the same outcome.

Built for long sessions

The game is meant to support growth over time, from early survival decisions to complex late-stage colony operations.
